You've just taken the first step toward AI that actually remembers your work.

Most AI tools forget everything the moment you close a tab. Souvenir is different – it keeps your context, your workflows, and your progress intact, so you can pick up exactly where you left off.

Here's how to get started:

  • Open your workspace
  • Add your first context (project, idea, or conversation)
  • Try switching between models – your context stays with you

What to expect

Souvenir is built to make AI feel continuous – not transactional.

Over time, it will:

  • Remember how you work
  • Organize your outputs
  • Turn repeated tasks into reusable workflows
